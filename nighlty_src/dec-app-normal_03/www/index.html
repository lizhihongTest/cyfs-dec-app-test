Web3 starts with CYFS

CYFS is the next-generation decentralized technology to build a Web3 with complete decentralization and freedom by upgrading the basic protocol of Web (TCP/IP+DNS+HTTP). With CYFS, everyone will be able to own their assets, data and applications.

  • You can speak
    freely and be true
    to yourself.

    The platform will not ban any account because you no longer rely on any centralized server. You are the only one to manage the account, and users can share their unique views on things or chat about their daily life at will.

  • You can naturally own your data.

    Have you ever been in trouble by the loss of personal data information due to problems such as service downtime and data inaccessibility? In the future, your data will store securely in your own device, or a device you trust, and the rights to the data belong to you only.

  • You can enjoy any services permanently.

    We collapse the traditional Internet model, which means that all application data transferred by your personal sever in CYFS ecosystem. As long as your personal device exists, application services will never shut down.

  • You can value
    your data.

    Your data will be able to "make money" for you from now on, and every piece of your data is a valuable NFT. Anyone can use their digital currency to reward the articles, pictures, videos, and anything you shared—also the right to transfer the benefit to others.
